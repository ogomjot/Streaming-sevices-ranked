¤ Hulu

Hulu is a leading U.S. streaming service, launched in 2008. It offers a mix of live TV, on-demand shows, movies, and original content. Known for providing current TV episodes shortly after they air, Hulu also produces acclaimed originals like The Handmaid's Tale.

Matto and Vision:
 Hulu's motto is "TV on your terms," focusing on flexibility and convenience for viewers.

Original Programming and Works:
 Hulu stands out with its extensive library of TV shows, movies, and live TV options. It offers ad-supported and ad-free plans, catering to different user preferences.

Technology and User Experience:
 The platform provides personalized recommendations, multiple user profiles, and flexible subscription options, including bundles with Disney+ and ESPN+.

Impact on the Entertainment Industry:
 Hulu has significantly impacted TV consumption by combining live TV with on-demand content, making it a key player in the streaming landscape.
